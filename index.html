Attractive charts for your new webapp or design mockup

Three key features of Chartmander.js

Interaction

Explore your data. Touch it, hover it. Your data will show up in a nice tooltip. Tooltip is painted on cavas. For richer styling options a html/css based tooltip is planed.

Animation

Drag attention of your users. With chartmander you can aniamte your charts on load and on update. Choose easing function from a wide range.

Customization

This library was built with attention to design. Customize points on line chart, donut hole in pie chart or switch between stacked or grouped bar chart.
